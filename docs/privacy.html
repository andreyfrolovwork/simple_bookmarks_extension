Privacy Policy — Bookmark Manager

Last updated: March 2025

Data collection

Bookmark Manager does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All operations are performed locally in your browser.

Bookmark data

Bookmarks are managed through the Chrome Bookmarks API. Data (URLs, titles, folder structure) remains on your device and is never sent to third parties.

Local storage

The extension uses browser localStorage only to save your theme preference (light/dark mode). This data stays on your device.

Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue at the extension's GitHub repository.
